Singulart Saatchi Art

Born in an artistic family in Hanoi (Vietnam), Thư Anh Nguyễn inherited a passion for drawing and painting from her mother and grandfather: two well known Vietnamese artists. She developed her skills from an early age and later graduated from Vietnam University of Fine Arts with a Bachelor degree. She took part in numerous international exhibitions, and her works were displayed in Paris, Venice, Budapest, Miami, Cusco and Hanoi, her beloved home city.

Now, living in Biel/Bienne (Switzerland), Thư still embraces her Vietnamese cultural inheritance. She paints unique creations carrying exotic moods and lively colors, reflecting her own emotions and harmony between East and West.
